The Prayer, The Stranger, and the Promise

In December 2021, I knelt down in prayer with one clear request: “Lord, let me meet my husband in 2022.” I didn’t know how it would happen, or where, or when. I only knew that I believed it would.

January 2022 arrived, and I was in full-blown mission mode — not for love, but for leadership. I was on the hunt for new members for my cell group at church, fueled by purpose, not romance. That Sunday, while scanning the congregation, my eyes landed on two young men seated quietly at the back. One of them had a quiet confidence about him — calm, observant, slightly mysterious.
